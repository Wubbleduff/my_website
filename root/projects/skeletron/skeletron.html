Skeletron

The Game

Skeletron is a first person shooter where the player runs through a neon-stylized world, shoots skeletons, and defeats the final boss. Make sure you don't run out of ammo - each shot bullet must be retrieved!

The Team

"Moone" is a team of 6 people. There are 3 programmers, 2 designers, and 1 audio designer.

Development

Skeletron is being developed using Unity and Unity's HDRP to create stylized graphics.
